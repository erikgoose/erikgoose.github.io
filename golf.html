Erik Gustafson - Golf

My Golf Career

When I was just 3 years old, I hit my first bucket of balls and embarked on my golf journey. That early start fueled a passion that grew as I swung my way through childhood, learning more about the game and refining my skills. Each game on the green brought new challenges and joys, shaping my love for golf that continues to flourish today. Now, I am proud to work at Riviera Country Club in Coral Gables, where I continue to share my passion and expertise in golf.
